About Computer Partner

We've been providing innovative computing solutions since 2005

Our Story

Computer Partner was founded in 2005 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality computing solutions that empower businesses and individuals to achieve more. What started as a small operation with just three employees has now grown into a leading technology provider with over 200 team members across multiple locations.

Our journey began when our founders, tech enthusiasts with decades of experience, noticed a gap in the market for personalized, expert-driven computing solutions. They envisioned a company that would not only sell hardware and software but also provide comprehensive support and guidance.

As technology evolved, so did we. From desktop computers and basic networking solutions, we expanded our offerings to include cutting-edge servers, cloud services, cybersecurity solutions, and custom software development. Throughout our growth, we've maintained our commitment to personalized service and technical excellence.

Today, Computer Partner stands as a trusted name in the industry, serving clients ranging from small businesses to large enterprises across various sectors. Our success is built on our ability to adapt to changing technologies while staying true to our core values of integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

At Computer Partner, our mission is to empower organizations and individuals through innovative technology solutions that drive efficiency, growth, and success. We strive to deliver reliable products and services that meet the evolving needs of our clients while providing exceptional support and expertise.

We believe in creating lasting partnerships with our clients by understanding their unique challenges and delivering tailored solutions that address their specific requirements. Our goal is not just to sell technology but to help our clients leverage it effectively to achieve their objectives.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted and respected technology partner for businesses and individuals, known for our technical excellence, innovative solutions, and outstanding customer service. We aspire to be at the forefront of technological advancement, constantly exploring new possibilities and bringing cutting-edge solutions to our clients.

We envision a future where technology is seamlessly integrated into every aspect of life and business, enhancing productivity, creativity, and connection. We aim to play a pivotal role in this transformation by providing accessible, reliable, and forward-thinking computing solutions.
